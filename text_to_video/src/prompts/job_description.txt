Summer Intern/Co-op 2026 – Agentic AI Developer 
About the job
Our Agentic AI Developer Internship offers an exciting opportunity to strengthen your engineering and architectural skills while gaining hands-on experience in building AI-driven solutions.

Position Responsibilities

Develop innovative agentic AI applications that support our Business, Operational, and Technical teams.
Work toward bringing these applications to production, gaining practical experience with cutting-edge technologies.

Required Qualifications

Currently pursuing an undergraduate degree in Computer Science, Engineering, or a related field.
Experience with Azure, React, OpenAI LLMs and SLMs.
Experience with Python, FastAPI, and vector databases, including building and managing databases.

Preferred Qualifications

Strong, clear, and efficient communication skills.
